Area Woman Complains Giant Spiders Ruining Vibe

Gloamfield local Clairy Osur has spent the last month protesting in front of town hall. At issue is the presence of the native giant spiders of the Webwood. Standing outside her home in the Webwood's biggest town, she gestures to the famous webs that gave the region its name. “It's just awful!” she complains. “I moved here looking for a quiet life in the forest. Instead I can't go frolicking in the woods because of spiders bigger than I am!”
We asked the provincal government, local government, and the Silk Farmer's Union for comment, but none responded.

Local Business Watches Competitor Make Mistake, Decides It Will Go Better for Them

Enchanting giant Blue Knight Inc. has announced they will be adopting the very same automated rune-crafting system that saw their biggest competitor, collapse earlier this year. “It's been seven months,” Blue Knight CEO says. “It'll be fine. Auto-crafters have been around long enough we know what happens when they're introduced to a business.” Expeditions Enchanted, who filed for bankruptcy earlier this year after adopting this technology lead to a customer revolt has simply said, “We wish BK all the best and hope this works out for them.”
